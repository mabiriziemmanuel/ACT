Programme of the Event

Come One! Come All!

ACT Vision Bearer

Mabirizi Emmanuel

Mabirizi Emmanuel is a dedicated God natured servant leader with a vision to transform communities around him with the support of willing community members, volunteers and any other donors across the globe. He started down from earth helping very small villages blossom up by engaging people into environmental sanitation, and now he is targeting the entire Uganda, and the world at Large. With a background in community development and youth empowerment, Emmanuel brings fresh ideas and energy to an empathetic landscape.


"ACT MARATHON" is inspired by the power of collective action. We believe that citizens can drive positive change in their communities through collaborative efforts.


Our initiative, led by Mabirizi Emmanuel, aims to harness the energy and enthusiasm of individuals from all walks of life to address critical needs, starting with access to clean water. By coming together in a marathon, we're not just promoting physical health but also community development and empowerment. ACT stands for citizens taking charge of their own development, and we're excited to see the impact we can make together." - A COMMUNITY TRANSFORMED

Our Priorities this Year

Health Empowerment Marathon

This is a dynamic initiative designed to energize communities through movement, education, and collective action. By participating, you’re not just improving your own wellbeing; you’re helping fund local health programs and spread awareness about preventive care. Together, we’re proving that health is a journey best traveled side by side. Whether you’re running, volunteering, or cheering from the sidelines, your involvement fuels a brighter, healthier future for all. Join us and turn momentum into meaningful change!

Construction of wells

The impact of a single well ripples far beyond its immediate surroundings. Children spend less time fetching water and more time in school, families enjoy better health, and entire villages gain resilience against droughts. By investing in well construction, we’re not just digging holes in the ground—we’re laying the foundation for stronger, healthier, and more self-sufficient communities. Every well represents hope, dignity, and a sustainable future for generations to come.
