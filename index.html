About Brother Mabirizi Emmanuel

Committed to Our Communities

Mabirizi Emmanuel is a dedicated servant leader with a vision to transform Busiro East into a model constituency. He started down from earth helping very small communities spring up and now he is targeting the entire Busiro East, Uganda and The world at Large. With a background in community development and youth empowerment, Emmanuel brings fresh ideas and energy to the christian empathetic landscape.

His efforts focuses on education, healthcare, infrastructure development, and economic empowerment for all residents of Busiro East. Emmanuel believes in transparent, accountable leadership that puts the people first.

As a Master Guide in the Seventh-day Adventist Church, Emmanuel's voluntary character is rooted in strong moral values and service to humanity. His motto "Kulwa Busiro Empya" reflects his commitment to creating a new, better Busiro East for all relying on the community support and Donations.

Our Priorities this Year

Health Empowerment Marathon

This is a dynamic initiative designed to energize communities through movement, education, and collective action. By participating, you’re not just improving your own wellbeing; you’re helping fund local health programs and spread awareness about preventive care. Together, we’re proving that health is a journey best traveled side by side. Whether you’re running, volunteering, or cheering from the sidelines, your involvement fuels a brighter, healthier future for all. Join us and turn momentum into meaningful change!

Construction of wells

The impact of a single well ripples far beyond its immediate surroundings. Children spend less time fetching water and more time in school, families enjoy better health, and entire villages gain resilience against droughts. By investing in well construction, we’re not just digging holes in the ground—we’re laying the foundation for stronger, healthier, and more self-sufficient communities. Every well represents hope, dignity, and a sustainable future for generations to come.
